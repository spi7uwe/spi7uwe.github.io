About This Project

A living space for Secwépemctsín language, culture, and continuity.

What this site is

This site is dedicated to Secwépemctsín, the language of the Secwépemc people.

It exists to support speaking, listening, and remembering through real words, real voices, and real stories. Secwépemctsín is treated here not as an academic subject, but as a living language meant to be spoken, heard, and carried forward.

Language and awakening

Secwépemctsín-kt wel me7 yéws.

We will speak Secwépemctsín forever.

Language carries more than vocabulary. It carries ways of thinking, ways of relating, and ways of knowing who we are.

By speaking Secwépemctsín, we awaken memory — cultural, ancestral, and personal. This awakening happens through sound, repetition, story, and everyday use.

Culture through story

Stories are one of the oldest ways language is held and passed on.

The lessons and story recordings on this site are meant to be listened to, returned to, and spoken aloud. Through story, language stays connected to land, teaching, and lived experience.

How to use this space

  • Listen before reading
  • Speak the language out loud
  • Do not worry about perfection
  • Return often — language grows with use

Ongoing

This site will continue to grow with more lessons, stories, and recordings.

It is intentionally simple, so it can remain accessible and usable for future generations.
