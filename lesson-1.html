Lesson 1 — Xwixwéye

Giant Maggot story

← Back to Lessons

How to use this lesson

  1. Listen first.
    Play the audio all the way through without reading. Let the sounds and rhythm settle in your ear.
  2. Listen again.
    This time, follow along with the Secwepemctsín text only. Do not worry about understanding every word.
  3. Speak out loud.
    Pause the audio and repeat each line. Focus on flow, not perfection.
  4. Listen a third time.
    Connect the sound, the text, and the meaning together.
  5. Return later.
    Come back to the audio on another day. Language grows through repetition over time.

This lesson is not meant to be rushed. Use it the way language is meant to be used: slowly, aloud, and often.

Audio

Story Text — Xwixwéye

Listen first. Then read. Speak it out loud.

Le q̓7éses lu7 w7ecekwe r xiymuy r xwixwéye. Kist r xwixwéye.

Long ago, they say, there was a big maggot. A very bad maggot.

Kwens xwexwéyt r stsillén ell r sewllkwe ell cw7it t̓e stém te tmicw.

He took all the food, the water and all the things from the land.

T̓7oy̓e r tmicw.

The land was lacking.

Illens r xwexweyt.

He ate everything.

Pell xwexweyt r stem.

He had everything.

Xwixwéye: “Oh le7 t’é stsillen! Me7 íllen ke xwexwéyt! Ch ch ch tstststs…”

“Oh, good food. I’m gonna eat it all.”
