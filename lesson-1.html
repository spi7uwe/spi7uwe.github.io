Story text — Xwixwéye

Listen first. Then read. Repeat out loud.

Le q̓7éses lu7 w7ecekwe r xyúmuwi r xwixwéye.

Maggot was travelling along with Coyote.

Kist r xwixwéye.

Maggot spoke.

Kwens xwexwéyt r stillén ell r sewllkwe ell c7úts̓e r stém te tmicw.

He talked about everything that lived and moved on the land.

T̓7oy̓e r tmicw.

The land was alive.

Illens r xwexweyt.

Everything listened.

Pell xwexweyt r stem.

Everything understood.

“Oh le7 t’é stillen! Me7 íllen ke xwexwéyt! numnumnumnum!”

“Oh how delicious! I will eat everything! numnumnumnum!”
