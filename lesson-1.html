Story Text — Xwixwéye

Listen first. Then read. Speak it out loud.

Le q̓7éses lu7 w7ecekwe r xiymuy r xwixwéye. Kist r xwixwéye.

Long ago, they say, there was a big maggot. A very bad maggot.

Kwens xwexwéyt r stsillén ell r sewllkwe ell cw7it t̓e stém te tmicw.

He took all the food, the water and all the things from the land.

T̓7oy̓e r tmicw.

The land was lacking.

Illens r xwexweyt.

He ate everything.

Pell xwexweyt r stem.

He had everything.

Xwixwéye: “Oh le7 t’é stsillen! Me7 íllen ke xwexwéyt! Ch ch ch tstststs…”

“Oh, good food. I’m gonna eat it all.”

Nə Secwepemcúl̓ecw
